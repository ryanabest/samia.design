Firefly

Concept

Moving into a different country, city, even a street can alienate any person. Searching up information to discover has too much friction. Through Firefly, any commuter can discover stories as they walked. It’s targeted to people who mostly walk as their means of transportation, which is a commonality within cities. Each walker is a firefly, and this app will allow them to discover their interests. It can also help tourists visiting a new city. Firefly is an interactive app which provides pedestrians the ability to actively, yet effortlessly, discover their interests while roaming cities, hopefully worldwide.

Mobile Application

It would be based on two main components; the geolocation and personal interests. The map will have it has colored markers to signify to walker the location of these potential discoveries. The walker must physically pass by the marker to discover. Once they reach it, they can then find out more with a short factoid with its source on the web.

After a couple of walks, it would collect discoveries, to which the user can organize them using the available filters. For those who have particular interests, they may search a topic to locate a maker for it as well. It will encourage the curious local or curious tourist to discover their surroundings by simply walking.
